The Ultimate 7-Day Kashmir Tour Itinerary (Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam)

Seven days is the sweet spot for a Kashmir vacation. It allows you to explore the "Golden Triangle" of Kashmir—Srinagar, Gulmarg, and Pahalgam—plus Sonamarg, without rushing. At AKTASHAF KASHMIR , we have crafted thousands of itineraries, but this 6 Nights / 7 Days plan remains our most popular bestseller.

Quick Itinerary Overview
  • Day 1: Arrival & Shikara Ride
  • Day 2: Srinagar to Sonamarg Day Trip
  • Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg
  • Day 4: Gulmarg to Pahalgam
  • Day 5: Explore Pahalgam
  • Day 6: Pahalgam to Srinagar (Houseboat)
  • Day 7: Departure

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Sunset Shikara Ride

Welcome to Paradise! Upon landing at Srinagar Airport, our representative will greet you. Check into your hotel and rest for a while.

  • Afternoon: Visit the famous Mughal Gardens (Nishat & Shalimar Bagh).
  • Evening: Enjoy a mesmerizing 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake during sunset.

Day 2: Day Trip to Sonamarg (Meadow of Gold)

After breakfast, drive to Sonamarg (80km, 2.5 hours). The drive along the Sindh River is breathtaking.

  • Activity: Hire a pony to visit the Thajiwas Glacier where snow remains year-round.
  • Return: Drive back to Srinagar in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers)

Drive to Gulmarg (50km, 2 hours), India's premier ski resort.

  • Main Attraction: The Gulmarg Gondola. It's the world's highest cable car. Phase 1 takes you to Kungdoor, and Phase 2 takes you to Apharwat Peak.
  • Stay: Overnight stay in a cozy hotel in Gulmarg.

Day 4: Gulmarg to Pahalgam (Valley of Shepherds)

A scenic drive to Pahalgam (140km, 4-5 hours). You'll pass through saffron fields and apple orchards.

  • On the way: Stop at the Avantipur Ruins and bubbling sulphur springs.
  • Arrival: Check into your hotel riverside hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Exploring Pahalgam

Pahalgam is the crown jewel of Kashmir's valleys.

  • Betaab Valley: Famous for Bollywood movie shoots.
  • Aru Valley: A quaint village perfect for short treks.
  • Chandanwari: The starting point of the Amarnath Yatra.

Day 6: Return to Srinagar - Houseboat Stay

Drive back to Srinagar and check into a luxury Houseboat on Dal or Nigeen Lake. This is a quintessential Kashmiri experience.

  • Evening: Shopping at Lal Chowk or relaxing on the houseboat deck.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, we will drop you at Srinagar Airport with a bag full of memories.
