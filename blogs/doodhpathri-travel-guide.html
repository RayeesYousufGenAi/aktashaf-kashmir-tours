Doodhpathri Travel Guide 2026: Exploring the "Valley of Milk"

If you think you've seen the best of Kashmir in Gulmarg and Pahalgam, wait until you visit Doodhpathri . Located just 42km from Srinagar, Doodhpathri (literally translating to "Valley of Milk") is an emerging tourist destination known for its velvety green meadows that look like European alps.

Why Visit Doodhpathri?

Unlike the crowded tourist spots, Doodhpathri offers raw, untouched beauty. The name comes from the Shaliganga river which crashes against rocks, creating a white foam that resembles milk.

  • Less Crowd: Perfect for peace lovers.
  • Picnic Spot: Locals love this spot for family picnics.
  • Scenic Drive: The route to Doodhpathri is as beautiful as the destination itself.

How to Reach Doodhpathri?

Doodhpathri is easily accessible from Srinagar airport or city center.

  • Distance: Approx 45km from Srinagar (1.5 - 2 Hours).
  • Route: Srinagar -> Budgam -> Khansahib -> Doodhpathri.
  • Taxi Fare: A round trip private cab costs between ₹2,500 - ₹3,500 depending on the season.

Top Things to Do

  1. Walk on the Meadows: The vast green bowl-shaped valley is perfect for barefoot walking.
  2. Visit Shaliganga River: Dip your feet in the ice-cold glacial water.
  3. Pony Ride: Ride a horse to explore deeper parts of the forest (scenic viewpoints).
  4. Photography: The landscape offers incredible contrast for photos.

Where to Stay?

Currently, accommodation options in Doodhpathri are limited.

  • JKTDC Tourist Huts (Need prior booking).
  • Camping (Bring your own tents).
  • Recommendation: It is best visited as a Day Trip from Srinagar. Stay in Srinagar and visit for the day.

Pro Tips for Travelers

  • Carry packed lunch/snacks, as there are fewer restaurants.
  • Wear comfortable shoes; you will want to walk a lot.
  • Network coverage can be spotty, so download offline maps.
