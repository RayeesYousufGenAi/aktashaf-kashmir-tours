Gulmarg, Pahalgam & Sonmarg: The Golden Triangle of Kashmir

No trip to Kashmir is complete without visiting its famous trio: Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonamarg. Each destination offers a unique slice of paradise, from snow-capped peaks to rushing rivers and golden meadows. Here is your ultimate guide on what to see and do in each of these stunning locations with AKTASHAF KASHMIR .

Explore the Trio
  • 1. Gulmarg: The Meadow of Flowers
  • 2. Pahalgam: The Valley of Shepherds
  • 3. Sonamarg: The Meadow of Gold
  • How to Travel Between Them?

1. Gulmarg: The Adventure Capital

Located 50km from Srinagar, Gulmarg is famous for having one of the world's best ski resorts and the highest golf course.

  • Gondola Ride: The star attraction. Phase 1 goes to Kungdoor, and Phase 2 goes to Apharwat Peak (4,200m). Pre-booking is essential!
  • Skiing: In winter (Jan-Mar), it's a skier's paradise with powder snow.
  • Drung Waterfall: A frozen waterfall nearby that's perfect for photos.

2. Pahalgam: The Serene Retreat

About 95km from Srinagar, Pahalgam is all about lush greenery, the Lidder River, and relaxing vibes.

  • Aru Valley: A scenic 12km drive from Pahalgam town, offering breathtaking views.
  • Betaab Valley: Named after the movie 'Betaab', it's a beautiful park surrounded by mountains.
  • Chandanwari: The snow point and start of the Amarnath Yatra.
  • Lidder River: Go for white water rafting or trout fishing.

3. Sonamarg: The Gateway to Ladakh

Located 80km from Srinagar, Sonamarg (Meadow of Gold) is known for its glaciers and proximity to Zojila Pass.

  • Thajiwas Glacier: A short trek or pony ride takes you to this glacier where snow exists even in summer.
  • Zero Point: An adventure drive towards Zojila Pass to see massive snow walls.
  • Camping: The best place in Kashmir for riverside camping.

How to Travel Between Them?

Srinagar acts as the central hub. Typically, itineraries follow this route:

  1. Srinagar -> Sonamarg (Day Trip or Overnight) -> Srinagar
  2. Srinagar -> Gulmarg (Overnight)
  3. Gulmarg -> Pahalgam (Direct drive approx 4-5 hours)
